About My Journey & Philosophy

A direct walkthrough of how I work, what drives my creative decisions, and why the brands I've built look and feel the way they do.

The story behind the work

Born in Kuwait and raised on the creative pulse of Alexandria, my path was carved by an artistic lineage — a family of musicians and artists, where rhythm and expression were the standard. My brother is Takki of the legendary band MTM — growing up in his orbit taught me a great deal about writing and music production. That talent was disciplined at university, where I majored in Graphics. Behind the lens of a Minolta film camera I found my first obsession: turning a mastery of photography into a craft that evolved from capturing light into styling and food photography.

Since 2007 I've worked across Egypt, Qatar, and the UAE — from PR at the Bibliotheca Alexandrina to social for Al Siddiqi's Doha hospitality group, Creative Content Director at Nineteen84 in Cairo, and now Head of Social at Tonic International, Dubai. I've been the Creative Director, the copywriter, the photographer, the strategist and the producer — often all at once.

At Tonic I also serve as Head of Artificial Intelligence, spearheading AI into our creative workflows — a TikTok-first, AI-driven production model that reached 15M+ views in a single quarter and builds digital-first brand mascots at a speed that used to require a full agency.

My approach

Today I've evolved from capturing noise to simulating it. I'm a strategist and a creator, driven by exploration — my work revolves around the depths of human psychology and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in digital storytelling.

Whether I'm automating workflows with complex systems for global brands, or producing the haunting, cinematic narratives that live on Escapegoat, a campaign or a track is never just content — it's a journey into the mind, designed to keep the industry on notice and my audience a step ahead of the future.

Spirit & the machine

A personal spiritual awakening rewired how I create — it taught me presence, patience and trust, and to quiet the ego long enough to listen. That's exactly why AI doesn't rattle me. I don't fear the machine; I collaborate with it — a creative partner that amplifies intention rather than replacing it. I bring the soul and the story; it brings the speed. Together we make work that's faster and more human — head in the clouds, hands on the craft.
