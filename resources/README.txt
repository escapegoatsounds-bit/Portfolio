RESOURCES FOLDER — ZIZO Focus App
===================================

music/
  Drop your own audio files here.
  Supported formats: .mp3  .wav  .ogg  .flac  .m4a  .aac
  The Focus App auto-detects files here and adds them to My Music
  on startup. No need to click Add for files in this folder.

photos/
  Profile headshots and project images for the portfolio site.
  Suggested names: headshot.jpg  hero.jpg  project-1.jpg
